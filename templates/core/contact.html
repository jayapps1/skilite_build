Support FAQ

Common Support Questions

How do I create a palette?

Open the Palette Editor. Select manual adjustments or trigger recommended configurations based on your business sectors.

What are preset libraries?

Presets are built-in defaults designed for construction, retail, software, etc. Click apply to copy the presets directly into your account.

How does publishing work?

Saved palettes can be marked as PUBLIC, shifting them into the Community Gallery where guest users can view, like, and copy them.

What export files are supported?

We support exports for CSS variables, SCSS variables, JSON, Bootstrap variables override, Tailwind blocks, React theme JS, and Android Compose files.
